Game Overview

Limited Defense is an engaging tower defense game that challenges your strategic thinking and resource management skills. Position your defenses wisely, upgrade your towers, and protect your base from increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

How to Play

  • Place towers strategically along the enemy path
  • Manage your resources wisely to purchase and upgrade towers
  • Adapt your strategy as new enemy types appear
  • Survive increasingly challenging waves
  • Upgrade your defenses to become more powerful

Game Features

Strategic Gameplay

Every decision matters in Limited Defense. Choose your tower placement carefully and plan your upgrades to create the ultimate defense system.

Multiple Tower Types

Experiment with different tower combinations to find the perfect strategy. Each tower type has unique abilities and upgrade paths.

Progressive Difficulty

Face increasingly challenging waves of enemies that will test your tactical skills and adaptability.

Resource Management

Balance your economy between building new towers and upgrading existing ones to maximize your defensive potential.
