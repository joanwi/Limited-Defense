Game Overview

World Z Defense is an intense tower defense game set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies. As the last line of defense, you must build and upgrade your fortifications to protect humanity from the relentless undead hordes. Each wave brings new challenges and increasingly dangerous zombie types.

The game features a wide variety of defensive structures and weapons, each with unique abilities and upgrade paths. As you progress through the levels, you'll face increasingly challenging waves of zombies. Use your strategic thinking to place the right defenses in the right locations and upgrade them at the right time to survive.

How to Play

  • Place defensive structures along the zombie path
  • Upgrade your defenses to increase their power and range
  • Earn resources by defeating zombies
  • Use different defense types to counter specific zombie types
  • Complete levels to unlock new maps and challenges
  • Master the art of defense placement and upgrade timing
