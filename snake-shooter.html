Game Overview

Snake Shooter is a unique twist on the classic tower defense genre. Instead of placing static towers, you control a powerful snake that can move around the battlefield, shooting at enemies and collecting power-ups. The game combines the strategic elements of tower defense with the dynamic movement of snake games.

As you progress through the levels, you'll face increasingly challenging waves of enemies. Use your snake's mobility to your advantage, dodge enemy attacks, and strategically position yourself to take out the most dangerous threats. Upgrade your snake's abilities to become even more powerful and survive the toughest challenges.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your snake's movement
  • Click or press space to shoot at enemies
  • Collect power-ups to enhance your snake's abilities
  • Upgrade your snake's stats between waves
  • Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves
  • Master the art of movement and shooting

Game Features

Dynamic Movement

Control your snake freely around the battlefield, dodging enemy attacks and positioning yourself for the perfect shot.

Power-up System

Collect various power-ups that enhance your snake's abilities, from increased damage to special attacks.

Upgrade System

Between waves, upgrade your snake's stats to become more powerful and better equipped for the challenges ahead.

Multiple Enemy Types

Face different types of enemies, each with unique behaviors and weaknesses that require different strategies to defeat.
