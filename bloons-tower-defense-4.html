Game Overview

Bloons Tower Defense 4 is a classic tower defense game that has captured the hearts of millions of players worldwide. In this colorful and engaging game, you'll use various monkey towers to pop balloons and prevent them from reaching the end of the path. Each monkey tower has unique abilities and upgrade paths, allowing for countless strategic combinations.

The game features a wide variety of balloon types, each with different properties and behaviors. Some balloons are faster, others are tougher, and some even split into multiple smaller balloons when popped. As you progress through the levels, you'll face increasingly challenging waves of balloons and need to carefully plan your defense strategy to succeed.

How to Play

  • Place monkey towers along the balloon path
  • Upgrade your towers to increase their power and range
  • Earn money by popping balloons
  • Use different tower types to counter specific balloon types
  • Complete levels to unlock new maps and challenges
  • Master the art of tower placement and upgrade timing
