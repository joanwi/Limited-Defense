Game Overview

Raid Heroes: Total War is an exciting tower defense game where you command powerful heroes to protect your territory. In this game, you'll build and upgrade various defensive structures to fend off waves of enemies. Each hero has unique abilities and upgrade paths, allowing you to create the perfect defense strategy.

The game features a wide variety of enemy types, each with different properties and behaviors. As you progress through the levels, you'll face increasingly challenging waves of enemies and need to carefully plan your defense layout. The game's strategic depth and engaging gameplay make it a must-play for tower defense fans.

How to Play

  • Place heroes strategically along the enemy path
  • Upgrade your heroes to increase their power and range
  • Earn resources by defeating enemies
  • Use different hero types to counter specific enemy types
  • Complete levels to unlock new maps and challenges
  • Master the art of hero placement and upgrade timing
