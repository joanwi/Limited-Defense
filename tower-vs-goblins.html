Game Overview

Tower vs Goblins is an engaging tower defense game where you must protect your kingdom from invading goblin hordes. As the kingdom's last line of defense, you'll need to strategically place and upgrade various towers to repel the relentless goblin attacks.

The game features a diverse selection of defensive structures, each with unique abilities and upgrade paths. As you progress through the levels, you'll face increasingly challenging waves of goblins with different abilities and behaviors. Use your strategic thinking to create the perfect defense and protect your kingdom.

How to Play

  • Place defensive towers along the goblin path
  • Upgrade your towers to increase their power and range
  • Earn resources by defeating goblins
  • Use different tower types to counter specific goblin types
  • Complete levels to unlock new maps and challenges
  • Master the art of tower placement and upgrade timing
